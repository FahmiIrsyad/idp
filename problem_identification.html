THE PROBLEM
  • Malaysia Priority Level

    With a per capita rice consumption of 78.9 kilograms annually, the nation's heavy reliance on rice underscores its significance.

  • Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO)

    Malaysia experiences a significant percentage of post-harvest losses in its paddy production, estimated at around 20-30%

    • Root Cause PHL in rice storage

  • Inadequate design and maintenance of storage facilities
  • Insufficient control over temperature and humidity levels
  • Lack of integrated pest management practices and monitoring systems
  • Lack of handling and packaging practices
